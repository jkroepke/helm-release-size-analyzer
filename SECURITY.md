# Reporting Security Issues

The community and I take security bugs seriously. We appreciate
your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort
to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security issue, please use the GitHub Security Advisory
["Report a Vulnerability"](https://github.com/jkroepke/helm-release-size-analyzer/security/advisories/new)
feature.

I will send a response indicating the next steps in handling
your report. After the initial reply to your report, the I will keep
you informed of the progress towards a fix and full announcement, and may ask
for additional information or guidance.

Report security bugs in third-party software to the person or team maintaining
the third-party software.
